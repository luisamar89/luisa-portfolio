Self Promotion | Editorial & typeface design

This is a mini book of a self designed typeface and mindfulness practices.

Mindful is the philosophy of putting all attention into the present moment observing all external and internal factors of the human being.

The typeface was inspired in geometric mandalas design because it represents the observation of mechanisms of nature and intern and extern structures of the human body.

I designed a mandala for each letter,
from A to Z. ​​​​​​​
