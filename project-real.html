Final Major Project | Editorial with Research Compilation

How do you know if THIS is real?

How can you be sure that you are awake or living in a dream?

How can you distinguish everything around you between reality, illusion or delusion?

Do we live in a multi dimensional universe?

The project is based on a research that includes articles of philosophy, science, religion and mathematics.

The idea is to try to answer this questions and ease or provoke curiosities about our inner and outer-self based on various world views.​​​​​​​

I designed a book with several edited and compiled articles. I also created a website in the final phase of the project as a (non)conclusion.
